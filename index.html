My Favorite Jazz Albums

by Chris

Some background:

I first heard jazz at a young age, but never really fell in love with it until a few years ago and now it's all I listen to. Here are some albums I recommend listening to:

(Click on the album art to open a new tab with a YouTube playlist for the selected album)

  • Reach for It (1977) by George Duke
  • Light as a Feather (1973) by Chick Corea and Return to Forever
  • Carnaval (1980) by Spyro Gyra
  • Open Mind (1984) by Jean-Luc Ponty
  • Soft Space (1978) by The Jeff Lorber Fusion
