The 5 Must-Listen to Jazz Fusion Artists

Here are some additional albums, all from several different artists, that I believe are individually great.

They are also generally jazz fusion albums which you may or may not enjoy more than those from the artists that I previously highlighted, so I list them as recommendations.

Much like before, you can click on either the album art or the name of best track from each album to open them in a separate tab to find some more great music.
