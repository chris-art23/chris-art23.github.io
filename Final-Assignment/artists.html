The 5 Must-Listen to Jazz Fusion Artists

Jazz Fusion is the coming together of jazz with rock, funk, R&B elements and, in theory, any other musical genre, such as latin, folk music, etc.

It came about in the late 1960s, reaching its maximum popularity throughout the 1970s with continuous experimentation taking place throughout the 80s, 90s and 2000s, with its most well known contribution being that it would evolve into Smooth Jazz.

Today we will highlight the five greatest Jazz Fusion artists, in my opinion, in no particular order. These are my personal favorites artists and we will also be highlighting my top five favorite albums from each artist and my favorite songs from each one.
